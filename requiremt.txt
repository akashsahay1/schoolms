# School Management System - Requirements

## 1. User Management
	1.1 Admin login with role-based access
	1.2 Student/Parent unique login credentials
	1.3 Password reset functionality
	1.4 Secure session management

## 2. Student Management
	2.1 Student registration with complete details
	2.2 Photo and document upload
	2.3 Auto-generate admission numbers and login IDs
	2.4 Class and section assignment
	2.5 Student search and filters
	2.6 Student profile management
	2.7 Student transfer and withdrawal

## 3. Class Management
	3.1 Create and manage classes (Pre-KG to 12th)
	3.2 Section management (A, B, C, etc.)
	3.3 Subject assignment to classes
	3.4 Class capacity settings

## 4. Academic Session Management
	4.1 Create and manage academic years (e.g., 2024-25, 2025-26)
	4.2 Set active academic session
	4.3 Session start and end dates
	4.4 Link students, fees, attendance to academic session
	4.5 View historical data by academic year
	4.6 Academic year-wise reports

## 5. Student Promotion
	5.1 Bulk promote students to next class
	5.2 Select students for promotion (pass/fail criteria)
	5.3 Retain students in same class (if failed)
	5.4 Transfer students to different section
	5.5 Graduate/Alumni marking for final year students
	5.6 Promotion history and logs
	5.7 Rollback promotion if needed

## 6. Fee Management
	6.1 Fee structure setup by class
	6.2 Fee types (admission, security, tuition, transport, etc.)
	6.3 Monthly/quarterly/yearly fee collection
	6.4 Fee receipt generation and printing
	6.5 Payment history tracking
	6.6 Outstanding fees reports
	6.7 Fee discounts and waivers
	6.8 Late fee calculation

## 7. Online Payment
	7.1 Integrated payment gateway for parents
	7.2 Parents can pay fees online via student portal
	7.3 Online/offline payment tracking
	7.4 Automatic receipt generation after payment
	7.5 Transaction logging and reconciliation

## 8. Examination System
	8.1 Exam schedule creation and management
	8.2 Marks entry by subject
	8.3 Grade calculation and report cards
	8.4 Result publication to student portal
	8.5 Rank calculation (class-wise, section-wise)
	8.6 Performance analysis reports
	8.7 Marks comparison across exams
	8.8 Print report cards

## 9. Attendance
	9.1 Daily attendance marking by class
	9.2 Subject-wise attendance (optional)
	9.3 Attendance reports (daily/monthly/yearly)
	9.4 Attendance percentage calculation
	9.5 Absent student notifications (SMS/Email)
	9.6 Attendance calendar view

## 10. Homework Management
	10.1 Create and assign homework by subject
	10.2 File attachments support
	10.3 Due date tracking
	10.4 Homework notifications to parents
	10.5 Submission tracking
	10.6 Homework history

## 11. Timetable
	11.1 Create class timetables with periods
	11.2 Teacher assignment to periods
	11.3 Period timing management
	11.4 View timetable on student portal
	11.5 Printable timetables

## 12. Staff Management
	12.1 Staff registration and profiles
	12.2 Staff attendance tracking
	12.3 Leave management system
	12.4 Department and designation setup
	12.5 Staff ID card generation
	12.6 Staff reports

## 13. Library Management
	13.1 Book inventory management
	13.2 Book issue and return
	13.3 Fine calculation for late returns
	13.4 Book search functionality
	13.5 Issue history tracking
	13.6 Library reports

## 14. Transport Management
	14.1 Vehicle and route management
	14.2 Driver details management
	14.3 Student-route assignment
	14.4 Transport fee management
	14.5 Route-wise student reports

## 15. Communication
	15.1 Notice board with email notifications
	15.2 SMS integration for important alerts
	15.3 Event calendar
	15.4 Photo gallery for events
	15.5 Circular management
	15.6 Parent-teacher messaging

## 16. Student Portal
	16.1 Dashboard for students/parents
	16.2 View student profile and details
	16.3 Pay fees online
	16.4 View payment history and download receipts
	16.5 View exam results and report cards
	16.6 View homework and timetable
	16.7 View attendance calendar
	16.8 View school notices and events
	16.9 Leave application submission
	16.10 Contact school feature

## 17. Reports & Analytics
	17.1 Daily/Monthly/Yearly fee collection reports
	17.2 Outstanding fees report
	17.3 Student list by class/section
	17.4 Attendance summary and analytics
	17.5 Exam performance reports
	17.6 Class-wise analysis
	17.7 Graphical dashboards
	17.8 Export reports to Excel/PDF

## 18. School Website
	18.1 Basic informational website
	18.2 About school, facilities, contact page
	18.3 Login links for admin and students
	18.4 Responsive mobile-friendly design
	18.5 News and announcements section
	18.6 Photo gallery
